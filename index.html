🩹 First Aid Information

Learn how to respond to high blood pressure, low blood pressure, elevated heart rate, and stress symptoms:

  • High BP: Stay calm, sit, hydrate, consult doctor
  • Low BP: Lie down, elevate legs, drink fluids
  • High Heart Rate: Relax, breathe deeply, check for medical issues
  • Stress: Deep breathing, meditation, rest, consult professional if needed
